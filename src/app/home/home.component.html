What is career guidance?

The term "guidance" first appeared in the 1530s and is defined as the process of directing behaviour. Career guidance is a comprehensive developmental scheme that assists individuals in making and carrying out informed educational and occupational choices. In a nutshell, it is a journey through which people learn to make mature and informed decisions. It is the act of directing or directing; it is the act of seeking advice. Individuals receive career guidance to help them acquire the knowledge, information, skills, and experience required to identify career options and narrow them down to make a single career decision. This career choice affects their social, financial, and emotional well-being throughout their lives.
